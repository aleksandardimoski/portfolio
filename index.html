Services

I handle it all, start to finish.

Editing is just one piece. From the first script idea to the final post, I handle every part: writing, design, and the content itself, so everything stays consistent and on-brand.

01

Script Writing

I write creative, effective scripts. A strong hook up front, and a structure built to hold attention to the end.

02

Video Editing

I cut videos with rhythm. Clean pacing, captions, and a style that actually fits your brand.

03

Graphic Design

I design clean, premium visuals. Thumbnails, on-screen graphics, and brand assets that elevate every post.

04

Social Media Management

I run your content calendars and posting strategy. Day-to-day management, so your brand shows up consistently.

How We Work

A calm, predictable process.

STEP 01

Script & hook

We lock the angle, the hook, and the structure, built to grab attention fast and hold it.

STEP 02

Footage & assets

You send the footage. I organize it, back it up, and gather the b-roll, graphics, and sound the script needs.

STEP 03

Edit, motion & sound

The edit comes together: pacing, animation, and a clean mix. Two rounds of revisions included.

STEP 04

Delivery

You get the final vertical video, plus any extra sizes or cutdowns you need, on time and ready to post.
